About Us

The store has been open for more than 30 years, when the founder saw the potential of selling items to build houses for households from poor to well-off.


Our store offers a wide range of windows and doors, old and new, at affordable prices. Convenient for customers who want to buy them for the purpose of repairing or building a new home. Until now, the store has provided a lot of products that go to every home, every store and a few other cities both at home and abroad.


You can go to the store to choose or order the items you want and we will deliver them to you. In addition, for items that are not available, we can search and notify you as soon as possible. Our store wants to bring the best to our customers.
